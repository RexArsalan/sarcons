About Us

Established in 2009, Sarcons Engineers & Contractors is a premier, multidisciplinary construction contracting firm with a strong footprint across India. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Abdul Rahman Shaikh, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality, cost-effective solutions that stand the test of time.

From residential and commercial buildings to large-scale public and industrial projects, we bring decades of combined expertise to every project. Our experienced teams leverage modern technology and best-in-class practices to ensure safety, sustainability, and superior craftsmanship at every stage of construction.
