About Us

Established in 2009, Sarcons is a premier construction contracting firm with a footprint across India. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Abdul Rahman Shaikh, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality, cost-effective solutions that stand the test of time.

From residential and retail buildings to public projects, we bring decades of combined expertise to every project. Our experienced teams ensure safety, reliability, and superior craftsmanship at every stage of construction.
